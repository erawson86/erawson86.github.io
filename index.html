About

I am a video producer and director specializing in corporate, aviation, agriculture, healthcare, and documentary storytelling, with experience in short and feature films. With experience producing content for Fortune 500 companies, agricultural enterprises, non-profits, and healthcare organizations, I bring creative vision and technical expertise to every project.

From aviation industry leaders like FlightSafety International to agricultural operations, economic development campaigns, and healthcare initiatives, I've helped organizations tell their stories through compelling visual narratives. Based in Kansas, in the heart of the USA.
